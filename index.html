Our Story

Mixtiles started in 2016 by two best friends, David and Eytan, who wanted to make wall decoration simple. Fast forward to today, together with our team of 50 super-talented designers, engineers, marketing, and operations experts, we’ve become the fastest growing company to have ever existed in our industry. Our vision forward is to deepen our existing reach, and to expand into new product categories, turning our wild crazy dreams into reality. That’s more or less it.
